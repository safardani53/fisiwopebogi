Photos Of Connie Brittons New Drama Series Hollywood Life

‘Dear Edward’ is a new Jason Katims drama series, adapted from Ann Napolitano’s bestselling novel of the same name. The series follows Edward Adler, a 12-year-old boy who survives a devastating commercial plane crash that kills every other passenger on the flight, including his family. As Edward and others all over the world are affected by the tragedy and try to make sense of life after the crash, unexpected friendships, romances, and communities are formed.

Colin O’Brien stars as Edward in a truly breakout performance. The series also stars Connie Britton, Taylor Schilling, Amy Forsyth, Anna Uzele, Audrey Corsa, Brittany S. Hall, Carter Hudson, Dario Ladani Sanchez, Douglas M. Griffin, Eva Ariel Binder, Idris DeBrand, Ivan Shaw, Jenna Qureshi, Khloe Bruno, and Maxwell Jenkins. ‘Dear Edward’ premieres on February 3 with the first 3 episodes, followed by new episodes weekly.
