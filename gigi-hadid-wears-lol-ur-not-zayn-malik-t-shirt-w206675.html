Gigi Hadid Wears 'LOL Ur Not Zayn Malik' T-Shirt

Saying it with her T-shirt! Gigi Hadid wore her heart on her sleeve, sort of, as she exited a Saturday Night Live afterparty at Dave & Buster’s in NYC on Sunday, May 15, wearing a shirt with boyfriend Zayn Malik‘s name on it.

Hadid’s cheeky tee was captioned with the phrase, “lol ur not zayn malik.” She paired the flirty white and black number with skintight lace-up leather pants, metallic pumps, a gunmetal clutch and a black choker necklace.

The Maybelline ambassador, 21, rounded out her rocker vibes with minimalistic makeup — a nude lip, strong brows and a subtle eye — and soft, beach-waved tresses, parted in the middle.

In response to his love of seven months’ fashion statement, Malik posted a pic of Hadid on Twitter, writing, “Thas ma girl.”

The couple first began dating in November 2015, have already appeared in a steamy Vogue fashion shoot together and even attended the star-studded Met Gala 2016 arm-in-arm earlier this month.

Want Gigi’s tee? We shopped her look at Rebbubble for $25, but if you’re more of a Harry Styles fan, you can get the same shirt with his name for $18 at Wild Daisy.
