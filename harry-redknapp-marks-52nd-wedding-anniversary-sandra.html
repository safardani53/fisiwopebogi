Harry Redknapp marks 52nd wedding anniversary with wife Sandra by sharing adorable smiley snap

HARRY Redknapp marked his 52nd wedding anniversary to wife Sandra by sharing a loved-up snap of them together.

The happily married pair met at the age of 17 at a local pub and tied the knot four years later in 1967.

Celebrating their milestone, the 73-year-old took to Instagram to share the adorable snap, which saw him wrapping his arm around a beaming Sandra, 73.

Sandra looked glamorous in the shot in a tropical print midi dress while Harry kept it simple in a blue shirt with beige trousers.  

Sharing the photo with his 1.3million followers, the former football manager gushed about his other half in the caption and reminisced about their first dance at The Two Puddings pub in Stratford. 

The former King of the Jungle wrote: "Happy Anniversary Sarn❤️❤️ From our first dance in two puddings to 52 years later...

"I love you more now if that’s even possible❤️."

The couple welcomed two children during the course of their marriage, sons Jamie, 46, and Mark.

Former footballer Jamie was quick to comment under the heartfelt post and wrote: "Happy anniversary love you". 

While Harry's former I'm A Celeb camp mate Fleur East penned: "Love you both so much! Congratulations".
