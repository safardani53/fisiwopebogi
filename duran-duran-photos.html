Photos Of The Band Members Then &amp; Now Hollywood Life

Duran Duran are total 80s icons! As a group, the British stars sold over 100 million records through their slew of hits, which included “Hungry Like a Wolf,” “Girls On Film,” and “Rio.” They have also earned major accolades over the years, including two GRAMMY Awards, a lifetime achievement from the MTV VMAs, and a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. Solidifying their place in music history, Duran Duran was inducted into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame in Nov. 2022

On top, members Roger Taylor, Nick Rhodes, Simon Le Bon, Andy Taylor and John Taylor are seen posing for a photo in London, England in 1981. The group had just released their self-titled debut album that included lead single “Planet Earth” as well as one of their most iconic songs to date, “Girls on Film”, and are considered a pivotal group in the new wave sound.

In the bottom photo, the group played a set of their best hits at the Rock & Roll Hall Of Fame Induction ceremony. During their set, fellow honoree Dolly Parton joined the band on stage.
