This Morning presenter shake-up as Dermot O'Leary welcomes new host after 18 months

THIS Morning welcomed back an old face to our screens today - Mollie King.

The Saturdays singer joined Dermot O'Leary for Tuesday's instalment of the ITV show.

After a beautiful rendition of Silent Night performed by a group of school children at the start of the episode, the camera panned back on the This Morning studio.

Dermot was over the moon to see Mollie, 35, back presenting the show.

The singer revealed that she was last on This Morning a whopping 18 months ago.

Mollie joined the cast of the ITV programme back in 2018, but had taken a break after giving birth to her daughter in November last year.

Over the past few months, there have been a number of different faces have hosted This Morning after Phillip Schofield and Holly Willoughby stepped down from their roles.

Phillip was axed from ITV earlier this year after confessing to an "unwise but not illegal affair with a younger male colleague".

Then in October, Holly shocked fans when she revealed she was stepping down from the show.

The pair has presented the programme together from 2009.

Last week another presenter shake up was revealed when Rylan Clark revealed Christine Lampard will make a comeback.

Over the past few weeks Rylan has also been joined by Emma Willis on the show, as well as Cat Deeley.
