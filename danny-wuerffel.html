Who is Danny Wuerffel:

Danny Wuerffel is a famous Football Player. He was born on May 27, 1974 and his birthplace is Pensacola, FL. Danny is also well known as, Former professional American football player who is best known for his tenure as the quarterback for the University of Florida football team. He played for the Gators between 1993 and 1996. In 1996, as a senior, he won the Heisman Trophy as the nation’s most outstanding college football player.

Danny is originated from United States. In 2006, he was inducted into the Florida Gators’ Ring of Honor alongside his former coach Steve Spurrier. Also inducted were fellow former players Emmitt Smith and Jack Youngblood.
