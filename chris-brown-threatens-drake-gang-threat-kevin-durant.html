Warns Of Gang Violence After Kevin Durant Run-In Hollywood Life

So chilling! We thought Chris Brown, 27, was done with all that gang banging talk now that he’s a daddy, but he’s back at it, slamming nemesis Drake, 30, online. Since you can’t throw gang signs via emojis, he used words to make the threat at the “Hotline Bling” star for accidentally shoulder-checking Kevin Durant, 28. The run-in was caught on camera during a post-game interview with the forward after beating the Toronto Raptors during “Drake Night” Nov. 16. Breezy shared the comment on a pal’s Instagram post about the semi-altercation, and it’s really disturbing!

Chris wrote in the comments section,”On PIRU. EASTSIDE 500 blk on da fingers.” Piru is a Bloods street gang out of Compton and the latter part with the fingers seems to indicate he’s throwing up a gang sign. His pal Sinko, who wrote the original post, is a member of Chris’ OHB crew, which stands for Original Hood Bosses. Obviously Sinko isn’t a fan of Drake either, as he captioned the video with Kevin’s now epic comment, “I don’t give a damn about no Drake night!!!” along with a middle finger emoji.
