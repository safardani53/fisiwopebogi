Leanne Morgan Big Panty Tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Who is Leanne Morgan?

Leanne Morgan is famously known as a comedian who has been working in the industry for over 20 years.

She first rose to fame in the early 2000s after she moved to San Antonio, Texas, and gained access to comedy clubs, according to her bio.

Prior to that, she graduated from The University of Tennessee with a degree in Child and Family Studies and sold jewelry at home parties.

Leanne often posts about her life on social media, where she has over 1.5million followers across all platforms.

She can also be seen on popular talk shows like The View and The Kelly Clarkson Show.
