Grace Elizabeth Biography

Grace Elizabeth is an American model known for working with the prestigious Victoria Angel. She became a Victoria’s Secret Angel in 2019. She has also graced the cover of many Vogue magazines.

Grace Elizabeth: Early Life, Bio, Wiki

Grace Elizabeth Harry Cabe was born on March 18, 1997, in Lake City, Florida. She was born to father Arnold Cabe, and mother Toni Cabe. Her mother is battling stage 3 ovarian cancer.

Grace Elizabeth‘s Career

The 22 years old model was signed at the age of 16 by Next Model Management after her mother sent them some pictures of her in 2013. After the Next Model, she was selected by Next in New York.

She professionally started modeling in 2015 for brands like Guess and Polo Ralph Lauren in print campaigns.

Elizabeth made her debut in the runway in February 2016 for Belgian designer Diane von Furstenberg in New York.

CAPTION: Grace Elizabeth SOURCE: Instagram

Her breakthrough came in November 2016 after she made her first appearance in Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show and was announced as the brand PINK’s spokesperson.

She walked the Victoria Secret runway in 2017 and 2018 alongside top models like Gigi Hadid, Kendall Jenner, and Bella Hadid. She became a Victoria’s Secret Angel in April 2019.

Magazines

Elizabeth has been in the cover of Vogue seven times. Some of them are in Vogue Korea, Vogue Mexico, and Vogue Spain. She graced in the cover of her first American magazine, V Magazine, alongside Sam Smith.
