Usher: Engaged to Grace Miguel!

Usher is engaged to his longtime girlfriend and business partner, Grace Miguel! The two have been together since 2009, and decided to make it official.

“She’s happy but they’re trying to keep it quiet,” an insider says private couple.

The couple were spotted at the Golden Globes Art of Elysium Heaven Gala in L.A. on Saturday, with Miguel’s ring finger adorned with a giant diamond.

Usher’s fiancee’s ring is SO massive, the New York Post’s Page Six opines, “you wouldn’t think it was an engagement ring because it’s so big.”

So we’re guessing he didn’t skimp on the bling in other words.

The very private pair, who were also spotted ringing in the New Year together at E11even nightclub in Miami, appear to be quite smitten with each other.

"I have an incredible partner and manager," he told Billboard in October. "She has helped me through some of the hardest times in my life and my career."

"She’s someone who’s able to support and understand all of who I am. Not just as a performer, but as a humanitarian and a business man and as a person."

It will be the second marriage for the R&B superstar, who is the father of sons Naviyd Ely and Usher Raymond V by his first wife, Tameka Foster.

They were married, to the dismay of his fans, from 2007-09.
