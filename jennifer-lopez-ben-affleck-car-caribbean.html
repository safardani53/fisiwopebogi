Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck squeeze into tiny car on holiday in Caribbean

JENNIFER Lopez and Ben Affleck squeeze into a tiny car as they do a little shopping on holiday.

The superstar pair looked rather cramped in the Mini Moke after buying some goodies on the Caribbean island of St Barts.

Ben, 51, wore a white shirt and jeans and J-Lo, 54, sported a flowery blouse and white sunnies.

The pair carried shopping bags as they soaked up the hot weather in the island’s capital, Gustavia.

Jennifer has continued to look sensational, flaunting her age-defying figure in a see-through dress.

The pop superstar is one of the cover stars for Elle's Women in Hollywood December/January 2023 issue.

Jennifer previously opened up about how she manages to look so good and insisted a lot of hard work goes into maintaining her figure.

She said: "It’s no secret that fitness is a very important part of my life.

“I think there’s a positive correlation between exercise and mental health. 

“When you find a good balance through determination and focus, we’re naturally pushing to be the best version of ourselves.”
