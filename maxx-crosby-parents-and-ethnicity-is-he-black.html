Maxx Crosby, a 27-year-old American of white ethnicity, was raised by his parents, Bryan and Vera Crosby.

He spent his formative years in Detroit and Flint alongside his older brother, Myles Crosby and sister Milla.

Maxx Robert Crosby is an American football defensive end who was born on August 22, 1997, and he now plays for the Las Vegas Raiders in the National Football League.

He attended Eastern Michigan during his college football career from 2015 to 2018, where he was named to the First Team All-MAC in both 2017 and 2018.

In week one against the Denver Broncos, Crosby—selected by the Oakland Raiders in the fourth round of the 2019 NFL Draft—made his debut and contributed six tackles to the team’s 24–16 victory.

He was notably placed 59th on the NFL Top 100 Players of 2022 and awarded a second-team All-Pro edge rusher by the Associated Press in 2021.

Recently, Maxx Crosby became one of the finalists of Defensive Player of the Year. However, he received zero votes for the Defensive Player of the Year Award.
