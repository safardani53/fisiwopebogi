Pregnant Jessica Simpson Wears Colorful Bridesmaids Dress at Wedding

Bridesmaids, starring the Simpson sisters!

Jessica and Ashlee Simpson were both bridesmaids at the Saturday wedding of Lauren Zelman, their friend and former assistant, to TV actor Bret Harrison (Breaking In, V, Reaper).

And though Jessica, 31, is in the very late stages of her pregnancy, she fulfilled her bridesmaid duties for the weekend at the Parker Palm Springs alongside fiance Eric Johnson, Ashlee, 27, her beau Vincent Piazza, her son Bronx and their parents Joe and Tina Simpson.

“She was a good sport about being a bridesmaid even though she is so pregnant,” a source told Us Weekly. “It definitely wasn’t easy for her to get around and help the bride.”

For the ceremony, the Fashion Star mentor wore a pink bridesmaids dress (one matching the other women in the party) and was the last to arrive for the outdoor ceremony. With a little bit of help from a wedding coordinator, the singer walked down the aisle “very slowly” to avoid falling, an onlooker explains. See more photos from the wedding.

(Her adorable nephew Bronx, though at one point teary-eyed, was a crowd pleaser at the bash as a ring-bearer; groom Harrison’s one-time costar on The OC, actor Adam Brody, was also in the wedding party.)
