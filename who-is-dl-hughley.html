Who is DL Hughley?

Darryl Lynn Hughley, popularly referred to as DL Hughley, was born on March 6, 1963, in Los Angeles, California.

DL Hughley is a multi-talented celebrity who works as a radio host, stand-up comedian, political commentator, and actor.

He is best known for his role as one of the main characters in The Original Kings of Comedy, where he starred alongside veteran comedians such as Steve Harvey, Cedric The Entertainer, and Bernie Mac.
