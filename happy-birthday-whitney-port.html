Happy Birthday, Whitney Port!

Whitney Port turns 25 today. Happy birthday, girl!

Ever since we first got to know Whitney on The Hills, she’s been a favorite of THG’s. There’s a lot to love about the sweet, genuine, expressive SoCal cutie.

While she began her reality TV career as BFF and co-worker of Lauren Conrad, she soon moved on to her own show, The City, chronicling her move to NYC.

There, Whitney Port only endeared herself to us more, even if some of the men (Freddie Fackelmayer) and friends (Roxy Olin) in her life are exasperating.

Happy birthday, Whitney!

We look forward to watching her life and fashion career continue to evolve in the coming year(s), and can’t wait for the new season of her hit series.
