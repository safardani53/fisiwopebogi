Teen Mom star Javi Marroquin slammed by fans for dressing son Eli, 2, in 'creepy' Halloween costume

JAVI Marroquin skipped the cute costume for son Eli and opted, instead, to go straight for scary, dressing the toddler up as Pennywise the Clown from It and facing backlash from fans.

Although the costume was incredibly detailed, fans were anything but impressed by the terrifying tike's look.

Javi, 28, shared several photos of him in his Georgie costume with his son, revealing that he has a flare for horror already.

He captioned the photo series: "We all float down here.

"What a fun age! Eli saw most of his favorite Halloween characters and we went by Michael Myers house about 4x. Hope everyone has a safe and fun Halloween!"

One commenter wrote: "Scary af!"

Another added "Not ok" with a laughing emoji.

A third fan wrote: "This is terrifying!!"

"He's 2, right? How does he even know who Pennywise is?" a commenter questioned.

Others simply noted the costume was "creepy."

FREAKY FANATICS

Not everyone thought Eli's costume was inappropriate. Horror fanatics were impressed.

One commenter wrote: "How cute is he!!!!"

"He looks so cute," another fan wrote.

Another fan wrote: "Best costume I've seen this Halloween."

"Omg so cool. He is adorable," a follower added.

Another wrote: "Great costumes."

'NO BAD BLOOD'

Javi appeared to be trick-or-treating without Teen Mom 2 star Kailyn Lowry, Lincoln, seven, or her other children.

The pair recently put their differences aside publicly, after a rocky co-parenting journey.

Javi and Kailyn, 29, each posted a photo posing with their son, who appeared decked out in a football uniform.

The reality stars posed on either side of the boy with a hand on each of his shoulders.

Javi captioned the post: "At the end of the day, we [him and Kailyn] do it all for you @lincmarroquin no bad blood, no grudges, no anger."

He went on to talk about the pride he experiences from coaching his son in football.

Javi also included kind words for his ex-wife, writing: "so thank you kail for taking care of the behind the scenes work so I can focus on the boys."

He concluded his message by writing: "Currently cit at 8-0 and excited to make the last push for the playoffs starting next week."

Fans were thrilled to see Javi and Kailyn getting along.

One person commented: "This is what it's all about Javi!

"TEAM WORK makes the DREAM WORK love seeing you both get along and actually giving kail some kudos!

"That's a man!"

WORKING TOGETHER

Kailyn previously said that Javi "turns off bulls**t for Lincoln, though she admitted that co-parenting isn't easy.

She opened up about the photo of her and Javi together on her podcast, Coffee Convos, saying: "It was important to me to get a shot of the 3 of us for @lincmarroquin because I want him to always know that no matter how much Javi & I got through off the field, we turn the bulls**t off when it comes to him.

"I think we've done a damn good job running our team this season & I cannot wait to see what we do next season!"
