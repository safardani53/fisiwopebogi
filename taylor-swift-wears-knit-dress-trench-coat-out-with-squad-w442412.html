Taylor Swift Wears Knit Dress, Trench Coat Out With Squad

Yep, the whole squad’s ready for fall! Taylor Swift and her famous friends — including Cara Delevingne, Suki Waterhouse and Lorde — whipped out their best autumnal threads for dinner at the Waverly Inn in NYC on Tuesday, September 27.

The “Blank Space” singer, 26, lead the party in the ribbed knit Elizabeth and James Penny Dress ($325, bergdorfgoodman.com), topped with a taupe Soia & Kyo Ornella trench coat. She finished her ensemble with a black Etro bag and peep-toe ankle booties.

Just as seasonally appropriate, Delevingne, 24, modeled a black crop top, high-waisted pants and a blue bomber jacket with a leopard purse and platform booties. Waterhouse, 24, chose a long-sleeved gray tee, black pants, and a short-sleeved draped sweater. And Lorde, 19, opted for a plunging, polka-dotted dress with ruffled accents, paired with a saddle purse and heeled brogues.

But it wasn’t just the usual squad members in attendance! The ladies were joined by Sienna Miller (in a three-quarter-sleeve top, high-waisted pants and sandals) and Brooke Shields (in a tunic and flared jeans).

The whole group was all smiles in and out of the posh West Village establishment — and yet, there was a little trouble that bubbled to the south. Kanye West stopped at Nashville, Swift’s honorary hometown, during his Saint Pablo Tour and played “Famous” — the song they’ve been feuding about for the better part of a year — three times. “Famous” infamously references the “Wildest Dreams” singer with the lyrics, “I feel like me and Taylor might still have sex / Why? I made that bitch famous.” Swift publicly took offense to being called a “bitch,” though, months later, Kim Kardashian released part of a recorded phone conversation that seems to suggest that the pop singer approved of the lyrics in advance.
