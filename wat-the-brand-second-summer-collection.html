Celeb fave WAT the Brand has released their second summer collection and we're obsessed

LOOKING for a wardrobe update this summer? You can't go wrong with WAT The Brand.

The new online label, which stands for We Are Twinset, has just released their second summer collection and it's a must for fashion fans, and has been been a huge hit with celebs too.

WAT The Brand, co-founded by Sarah Ellis and Philippa Ross in 2021, is the go-to for all fashionable garments.

It's quickly garnered a number of celeb fans too, including Rosie Fortescue, Christine Lampard, Louise Roe and Lydia Bright to name a few.

One year on from the launch and WAT The Brand has launched a second summer collection, also known as Marrakesh 2.0.

The new drop features a range of bestselling linen shirts and short sets, which you can mix and match, or wear together.

The new drop has a stunning new linen knot detail halter top, as well as linen trousers.

We're also loving WAT The Brand's tiered dress, which is the one piece we think we will live in this summer, and every summer holiday.

The 36-piece Marrakesh edit also includes swimwear, and beach cover-ups so you can feel comfortable and look a million dollars on your holiday.
