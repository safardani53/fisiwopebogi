Cruel mum from hell 'filmed herself sexually abusing her own children and uploading the sick footage

A MONSTROUS mum could get life in prison for filming herself sexually assault three of her own children and uploading the video to a paedophile website.

The Australian woman, who cannot be named for the protection of her children, pleaded guilty to 26 counts of sexual intercourse with a person under the age of 10.

The twisted parent appeared in Newcastle Local Court via video link from jail last week, reports The Sydney Morning Herald.

She pleaded guilty to further counts of producing child abuse material using a child under the age of 14 as well as inciting an indecent act on a person under 16.

After the footage appeared on a child pornography site, detectives reportedly identified an accent in the video and tracked her to Australia, where Australian Federal Police located the source.
