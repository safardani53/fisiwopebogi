What is Andrew Tate's theme song 'Tourner Dans Le Vide'?

CONTROVERSIAL Influencer Andrew Tate shot to fame on Big Brother before becoming known on social media.

In 2022, he went viral after fans kept making TikTok memes about him, accompanied by the French pop song - Tourner Dans Le Vide.

What is Andrew Tate's theme song Tourner Dans Le Vide?

Tourner Dans Le Vide is an orchestral pop song by French singer Indila.

It was released on February 22, 2014, and is the second single from her first album, Mini World.

In English the title of the track means: Spinning In The Emptiness.

Other popular songs by the 38-year-old include: Dernière Danse, Love Story and Parle à ta tête.

The song isn't an official theme tune.

It seems to have become a trend on TikTok to add the track to Andrew Tate parody memes, but it's not clear why the track in question was chosen.
