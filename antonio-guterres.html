Who is Antonio Guterres:

Antonio Guterres is a famous Politician. He was born on April 30, 1949 and his birthplace is Portugal. Antonio is also well known as, Socialist politician who was the Prime Minister of Portugal from 1995 to 2002. His tenure in power was marked by increased welfare spending and the introduction of several progressive social security initiatives.
