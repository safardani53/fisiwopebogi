While the high-end version will set you back a whopping £1,890, the M&S dupe retails at a much more reasonable £79 - making it an impressive £1,821 cheaper than the real deal.

It's little surprise the M&S 'Cotton Rich Belted Longline Trench Coat' has been compared to Burberry's 'Mid-length Kensington Heritage Trench Coat.'

After all, it has the same timeless double breasted front and includes similar traditional features such as smart epaulettes on the shoulders, buttoned cuff straps and a detachable belt.

The bargain buy is made from 98% cotton making it both lightweight and breathable - perfect for the country's unpredictable weather conditions - and comes in a relaxed fit so you can still layer up underneath if needs be.
