New ‘Hobbit’ Poster — ‘Battle Of The Five Armies’ First Still Revealed

In the third film of the mega-popular franchise, Bilbo Baggins, Galdalf and the Company of Dwarves will fight to recover the Dwarf Kingdom of Erebor. In the last flick, dragon Smaug was moving to Lake-town in hopes of destroying it! 

It’s time for the good guys to fight to get it back.

The movie poster shows a fiery battle. Alongside the top of the poster, three words read: “THE DEFINING CHAPTER.” We can’t wait to see what Peter Jackson has done with this film! So far, all of the Hobbit movies have perfectly lived up to their massive hype. 

Rumor has it the first-ever trailer from the movie will be screened to audiences at Comic-Con this weekend in San Diego, California. 

‘The Hobbit’ Movie: Third Installment Of Trilogy Gets Big Name Change

The movie’s poster looks amazing, but the flick was actually supposed to have a different name! The movie’s original title was There And Back Again, but the director announced in April that it would change to The Battle of the Five Armies, according to Variety.
