Who is Bobby Flay dating?

BOBBY Flay is an American celebrity chef notably recognized as the host of several Food Network television shows.

During his time as a chef, he has been in several relationships, but now fans are wondering who the star is dating.

Who is Bobby Flay dating?

The 58-year-old celebrity chef revealed he's been seeing someone during a September 2021 appearance on the Today show to promote his book Beat Bobby Flay.

"My girlfriend doesn't eat meat so I had to adjust," he said while cooking vegetable chili.

In August 2022, Bobby Flay opened up to ET's Rachel Smith about his girlfriend, Christina Perez.

"They’re going great. I'm a very, very lucky person," Bobby said.

"Christina, she's just a light in my life. She's so sweet. She's so smart. She just enhances everything that I'm able to participate in with her. I mean, she's fantastic."

"For now, I like it exactly the way it is. I think Christina does too," said Bobby when asked about marriage.

Prior to his relationship with Christina, Bobby admitted he's been riding solo since his last serious relationship ended in 2019 during a February 2021 episode of his show, Beat Bobby Flay, telling host Fran Drescher, "I'm very single."

"She [Helene Yorke] kicked me to the curb,” he added, hinting that his last romance didn't end well.

Who are Bobby Flay's ex-wives?

Debra Ponzek

Debra Ponzek is a professional chef and executive chef at numerous restaurants.

She met Bobby Flay in June 1990 and the two were engaged within a matter of weeks.

The couple got married in May 1991 and both were nominated for James Beard Foundation Award in 1992, competing against each other for the title of Rising Star Chef which Ponzek ended up winning.

In 1994, she moved to Connecticut to open her own restaurant, Aux Délices, divorcing Bobby to do so.

Kate Connelly

Kate Connelly is a journalist and TV host, best known for hosting Food Network’s Robin Leach Talking Food show.

Before finding her feet as a presenter, Connelly had a professional modeling career, which she ditched in favor of uniting her love of cooking and journalism.

She met Bobby Flay in 1994 when he made a guest appearance on the Food Network show and married in 1995.

The two, who have a daughter together, founded several restaurants before divorcing in 1998.

Stephanie March

Stephanie March is a 49-year-old actress best known for her role as Alexandra Cabot in the NBC series Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

The Texas-born star of The Social Ones met Bobby while working on the set of Law & Order SVU in 2000, with the pair tying the knot in 2005.

She appeared on several of Bobby Flay's Food Network shows, including Boy Meets Grill, Grill It! with Bobby Flay, Iron Chef America, and Throwdown with Bobby Flay.

March and Flay divorced on July 17, 2015.

Who are Bobby Flay's ex-girlfriends?

Heléne Yorke

Heléne Yorke is a Canadian actress known for her work as a voice actor and her time on Broadway.

She has worked on the Broadway adaption of American Psycho as well as lending her voice on the small screen with television credits spanning from Family Guy to Bojack Horseman.

Yorke appeared on a 2017 episode of Beat Bobby Flay, alongside her celebrity chef partner, where Flay revealed he met his now-ex at a New York Knicks game in 2016.

The couple remained together until Yorke called it quits in 2019, with the chef claiming he was "kicked to the curb."

January Jones

January Jones is an actress and model, best known for her work as Betty Draper in Mad Men from 2007-2015 for which she was nominated for two Golden Globe awards.
