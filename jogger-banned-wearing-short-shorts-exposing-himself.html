A JOGGER has been banned from wearing short shorts after he exposed himself to schoolgirls more than 40 times on his daily runs.

He denied the five counts of exposure but did admit “my shorts are very short” after being arrested on May 4 last year.

He was found guilty of the offences between January 1, 2018 and May 3, 2021.

Brave young victims told the court of the sick acts with one saying she was “disgusted” by his behaviour.
