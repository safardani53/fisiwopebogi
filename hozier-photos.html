Hozier Pics Of Performances &amp; Concerts Hollywood Life

His name is Andrew John Hozier-Byrne — but you know him better as just Hozier, and you know him best as the voice behind the 2014 bop ‘Take Me To Church’. (Yeah, good luck getting that out of your head for the rest of the day!)

An Irish crooner, Hozier has enjoyed a rise to fame across the world with his music, with his 2019 duet with Maren Morris, ‘The Bones’, rising through charts after it’s debut and fans flocking to his shows wherever he goes.

While he’s been linked to Saoirse Ronan in the past, it’s never been confirmed that they’ve been a couple. Of course, that didn’t stop people from whispering after she starred in his music video for ‘Cherry Wine’.
