Does Chris Pine Have A Wife? Timeline Of His Relationship History Hollywood Life

  • Chris Pine is an American actor.
  • Chris is not married.
  • Chris has dated famous actresses like Annabelle Wallis and Olivia Munn.

Chris Pine, 42, is one of the most eligible bachelors in Hollywood! The Dungeons & Dragons actor has never been married, which is surprising considering how handsome and successful he is. But Chris has explored the dating scene over the years with other actors like Annabelle Wallis, Olivia Munn, and Zoe Kravitz. Chris is quiet about his dating life, but considering that he’s in the public eye, nothing really stays that private for someone as famous as Chris. Find out more about Chris’s relationship history and his dating life below!

Audrina Patridge

The Hills star Audrina Patridge revealed in 2021 that she briefly dated Chris in 2009 after they met at the ShoWest Awards. “[Chris] came up to me and introduced himself to me and gave me his number,” she recalled on The Hollywood Raw podcast. “And all the girls were kicking me under the table like, ‘Oh, my God. You don’t know who that is?’ I’m like, ‘No.’ They’re like, ‘That’s Chris Pine.’ I was like, ‘Oh, he’s hot,’” she added. Audrina revealed that Chris “didn’t like the whole paparazzi side” of their relationship, and so things “just kind of dissipated” between them. In her 2022 memoir, Audrina said, “I have nothing but fond memories of Chris. He was truly one of the most normal people I’ve ever met in LA — and I mean that as the highest compliment.”

Olivia Munn

Chris was linked to Olivia Munn for five months, starting in Sept. 2009 following her breakup with Bryan Greenberg. The two never walked a red carpet or made their romance official in public appearances. They went their separate ways in the winter of 2010, but not before one last romantic dinner together in January of that year.

Dominique Piek

Chris dated South African model Dominique Piek for less than two years, from late 2011 to early 2013. They made their red carpet debut as a couple at the 2012 Cannes Film Festival. ​The Wonder Woman: 1984 actor talked about the breakup in an interview with OUT in May 2013. “It’s really hard in our business to maintain something. For me, right now, it’d be really hard,” he said.

 Zoe Kravitz

Chris was first linked to Zoe Kravitz in Sept. 2014, when they were seen together at a Coldplay concert. They were seen out and about together a few more time in the following months, before they went their separate ways. They stayed friendly over the years and Chris even attended Zoe’s 2019 wedding to Karl Glusman.

Vail Bloom

In May 2015, Chris sparked dating rumors with soap actress Vail Bloom, after they were seen cuddling on a lunch date in Hollywood. The relationship seemingly fizzled out shortly after.

Sofia Boutella

Chris was linked to his Star Trek Beyond co-star Sofia Boutella in September 2016. Sofia shared a photo of the pair at the premiere of Chris’s movie Hell or High Water. “With this special special human dear to me at ‘Hell Or High Water’ London premiere ! So proud of him !” Sofia wrote. Over six months later, PEOPLE reported that Chris and Sofia attended the NYLON Midnight Garden party at Coachella together. The outlet claimed that Chris and Sofia were “flirty” with each other at the event. They seemingly went their separate ways sometime after that night.

Annabelle Wallis

Chris’s most recent public relationship was with British actress Annabelle Wallis. Their relationship was first reported by the press in April 2018. “Chris and Annabelle are dating! She was seeing someone else when they met,” an insider told Us Weekly at the time. “They kept it casual for the beginning. Chris was very attentive and wooed her to get her attention away from the other guy.” Chris and Annabelle dated for over three years, until their breakup was reported by the press in March 2022.
