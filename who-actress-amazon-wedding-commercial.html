Who is the actress in the Amazon wedding commercial?

AMAZON is one of the biggest companies in the world, with nearly 20 subsidiaries and its streaming service.

In February of 2022, Amazon dropped a commercial involving a bride and her bridesmaid.

Who is the actress in the Amazon wedding commercial?

The 2022 Amazon wedding commercial features two actresses - the bride, and a bridesmaid.

Played by Kristen Lundberg, the bride seems to experience cold feet before walking down the aisle.

Her bridesmaid, played by Dina Freberg, gives her a message of encouragement.

"Hey, I get it. Commitment can be scary," Dina's character starts.

"But not when you're saving up to 15 percent with Subscribe & Save at Amazon."

She continues: "And if things don't work out, you can always cancel."

Who is Dina Freberg?

Dina Freberg is an actress, writer, and comedian based in Los Angeles.

She is currently represented by Activity Talent LA, AltaMero Talent Management, and the PKA Agency.

According to IMDb, Dina has appeared in a few television shows and short films throughout her career, including:

  • Titanium (2016)
  • Georgia (2017)
  • Hummingbird (2018)
  • Chicken Girls (2021)
  • Being the Ricardos (2021)

She has also appeared in commercials for companies such as Google and SlingTV.

Who is Kristen Lundberg?

Kristen Lundberg started in entertainment around 2007, appearing in various commercials and stand-up comedy showcases.

She graduated from the Art Academy of Cincinnati with a Bachelor's degree in Visual Communications.

According to Kristen's website, she also works as an ice sculptor in Los Angeles.

The actress has appeared in several television shows and shorts, including Vaudevillans (2021) and Oddly Popular (2020).

A native of Dayton, Ohio, Kristen currently lives in California.
