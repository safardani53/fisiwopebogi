What is the Megan Thee Stallion knees challenge on TikTok?

THE challenge Knees Like Megan is a TikTok trend that many users started doing in early 2021.

The trend started when rapper, Megan Thee Stallion performed the movement on stage, garnering much attention from TikTokers. 

What is the Megan Thee Stallion knees challenge on TikTok?

The Megan Thee Stallion knees challenge is not as complex as some other trends users take to the platform.

Users are squatted down performing rigorous leg and buttock movements, requiring a good amount of physical strength. 

The movement also requires coordination as users need to move their hips, knees, and buttock while moving up and down to the beat of the song. 

When uploading the challenge to TikTok, users typically use the hashtag #kneeslikemegan. 

The trend seems to have started in April 2021.
