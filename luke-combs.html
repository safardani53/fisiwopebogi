Who is Luke Combs?

Luke Combs is an American singer-songwriter, best known for his debut studio album, ‘This One’s for You,’. Luke’s album received double platinum certification from ‘The Recording Industry Association of America’ (RIAA), platinum certification from ‘Music Canada,’ and gold certification from the ‘Australian Recording Industry Association’ (ARIA).

It also earned Combs a nomination at the 2018 ‘Billboard Music Awards for ‘Top Country Album’.

 In 2019, Luke Combs shared the ‘CMT Performance of the Year award with Leon Bridges at the ‘CMT Music Awards.’

On June 11, 2019, Combs was invited to become part of the weekly American country music stage concert, ‘Grand Ole Opry.’

Luke Combs started singing at a young age and continued practicing even after entering high school.

Luke Combs Career 

Luke Combs is a famous American songwriter.

In February 2014, Combs came up with his debut extended play (EP), ‘The Way She Rides.’ On July 1, 2014, he released his second EP ‘Can I Get an Outlaw.’

Combs’ popularity increased in 2015 when his debut single ‘Hurricane’ peaked at number 46 on Billboard’s ‘Hot Country Songs’ chart.

He released his third extended play ‘This One’s for You’ in November 2015. Subsequently, he signed a deal with ‘Sony Music Nashville,’ which re-released his single ‘Hurricane’ through ‘Columbia Nashville.’

In 2016, he appeared in the music video of ‘Hurricane,’ which was directed by Tyler Adams.

On June 2, 2017, Combs released his debut studio album ‘This One’s for You’ through ‘Columbia Nashville.’

Upon its release, the album debuted at number five and number one on ‘US Billboard 200’ and ‘US Top Country Album,’ respectively.

In 2017, Combs was nominated at the ‘CMT Music Awards for ‘Breakthrough Video of the Year’ for his music video ‘Hurricane’ and also won the ‘New Artist of the Year’ award at the 2017 ‘Country Music Association Awards.’

In the same year, he also released the music video for his debut album’s second single ‘When It Rains It Pours.’

In 2018, ‘Hurricane’ was nominated for the ‘Country Song of the Year’ award at the ‘iHeartRadio Music Awards.’ At the same award ceremony, Combs won the ‘New Country Artist of the Year’ award.

At the 2018 ‘Billboard Music Awards,’ Combs was nominated for the ‘Top Country Artist’ award, while his album ‘This One’s for You’ received a nomination for ‘Top Country Album.’

At the 2018 ‘Country Music Association Awards,’ he won the ‘New Artist of the Year award and received a nomination for ‘The Male Vocalist of the Year award.

Meanwhile, he re-released ‘This One’s for You’ with many bonus tracks, including ‘Beautiful Crazy’ which peaked at number one on ‘Country Airplay Chart.’ ‘Beautiful Crazy’ received double platinum certification from ‘RIAA.’

He also released a music video for ‘Beautiful Crazy’ which was directed by Peter Zavadil.

In 2019, Luke Combs was nominated at the prestigious ‘Grammy Awards’ for ‘Best New Artist’ award.

In the same year, he also received a nomination for the ‘Video of the Year’ award at the ‘CMT Music Awards’ and won the ‘CMT Performance of the Year’ award which he shared with popular singer-songwriter and record producer Leon Bridges.

Combs came up with his fourth extended play ‘The Prequel’ which was released through ‘Columbia Nashville’ and ‘River House Artists’ on June 7, 2019. The EP, which has hit singles like ‘Beer Never Broke My Heart,’ debuted at number four on ‘US Billboard 200.’

It also peaked at number one and four in ‘US Top Country Albums’ and ‘Canadian Albums,’ respectively.
