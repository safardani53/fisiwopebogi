Aída Cuevas is a Mexican singer and actress. Affectionately known as, "The Queen of Ranchera Music", Cuevas has recorded 40 albums, selling 8 million copies worldwide. Cuevas' work has earned her one Grammy Award and one Latin Grammy Award. Cuevas' well-known songs are "El Pastor", "La Cigarra", "Te Doy Las Gracias", "Te Vas a Quedar Con Las Ganas", "Quizás Mañana", "Traición a Juan", "Me Equivoque Contigo", and "No Me Amenaces".
