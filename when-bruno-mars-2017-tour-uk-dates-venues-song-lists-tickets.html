When is Bruno Mars' 2017 tour? UK dates, venues, song lists and how to get tickets

BRUNO Mars' new album 24K Magic is released on November 18 and he will be hitting the road with a world tour in 2017.

The 31 year old Uptown Funk star has announced he is playing 10 dates in the UK and Ireland - so he will be definitely be bringing the magic to a venue near you.

 

What are the UK tour dates?

Kicking off in Glasgow, Bruno will be playing around the UK and Ireland before finishing up in Sheffield.

There are quite a few free days in the schedule so expect dates to be added if the demand is there.

The full list so far is;

April 12 - Glasgow, SSE Hydro

April 15 - Liverpool, Echo Arena

April 18 - London, O2

April 19 - London, O2

April 24 - Birmingham, BarclayCard Arena

April 27 - Nottingham, Motorpoint Arena

April 29 - Dublin, 3Arena

May 2 - Manchester, Manchester Arena

May 5 - Leeds, First Direct Arena

May 6 - Sheffield, Sheffield Arena

Is there a song list?

There is no song list at the moment, but as the tour is to promote his new 24K Magic album of course we can expect several tracks from it.

Legendary producer, Babyface is credited on the album and Mars says it's the best he's ever done.

Along with the lead single, Bruno has performed the upbeat track, Chunky -  and fans going to see him live can expect more of the smooth, funky groovy sound that he's made his own.

His last album, Unorthodox Jukebox won a Grammy, as did his smash hit of 2015 with Mark Ronson, Uptown Funk, so don't fear - he'll be playing some of his older hits too.

How do I get tickets?

Get ready as they are going to sell out fast!

Tickets go on sale on Monday November 21 and are available through Ticketmaster. Expect the prices to range between £42-£85.
