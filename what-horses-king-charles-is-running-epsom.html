What are the horses King Charles III is running at Epsom?

EPSOM festival starts on FRIDAY with two British racing classics on the agenda.

The 244th annual running of the iconic horse racing spectacle at Epsom Downs promises to be one to remember.

What horses is King Charles III running at Epsom?

King Charles III was due to run Reach For The Moon in the 12:50 Betfred Diomed Stakes at Epsom Downs.

John and Thady Gosden are the trainers of the four-year-old Bay Gelding.

However, the horse was not declared for the Diomed Stakes two days before the race is scheduled to take place, meaning it will not feature at Epsom Downs on Saturday.

It had also been rumoured that Slipofthepen, another horse owned by the King, was going to run in this year's Epsom Derby.

However, it has now been confirmed that King Charles will not have any runners at Epsom this year.

When is the Epsom Derby 2023?

  • The Epsom Derby will take place on Saturday, June 3rd.
  • It will begin at 1:30pm BST.
  • Epsom Downs will play host.
