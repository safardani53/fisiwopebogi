Kerry Lee aiming Top Gamble at the Topham Chase

KERRY LEE is set to run Top Gamble over the Grand National fences at Aintree for the first time in the Randox Health Topham Chase on April 13.

Lee's 10-year-old finished a fine third in a chaotic renewal of the Grand Annual at the Cheltenham Festival after meeting traffic problems.

Stablemate Gino Trail finished second in the finale of the Cheltenham Festival and will also be going to Merseyside, but in a different race.

Lee said: "Top Gamble is going for the Topham and, if the ground is right, Gino Trail will go for the Red Rum Handicap Chase. He ran in it last season (finished sixth) and the ground was too quick for him.

"I was very proud of both horses at Cheltenham.
