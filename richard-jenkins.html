Richard Jenkins Biography

Richard Jenkins was born in DeKalb, Illinois, and raised there. Mary Elizabeth (née Wheeler), his mother, was a housewife, and Dale Stevens Jenkins, his father, was a dentist. He went to DeKalb High School. Jenkins worked as a linen truck driver before becoming an actor (his boss was actor John C. Reilly’s father). Before moving to Rhode Island, he earned a bachelor’s degree in drama from Illinois Wesleyan University.
