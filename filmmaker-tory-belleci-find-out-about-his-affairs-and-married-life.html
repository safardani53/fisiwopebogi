Filmmaker Tory Belleci: Find out about his affairs and married life.

Tory Belleci, famously known for his show on the Discovery Channel called the MythBusters, is a Sicilian-American filmmaker and model maker. He is also known for his work with Industrial Light and Magic on films including Star Wars: Episode I- The Phantom Menace and Star Wars: Episode II-Attack of the Clones. The Federation battleships and pod racers are some of Tory’s creations.

Tory Belleci’s Affairs

That was a brief about his work but today we are here to take a peek into the personal life of this filmmaker. There were rumors flying around about Tory being gay. But he was quick to prove the allegations wrong by saying that he is straight and people spreading false news about him should get a life.

In this matter, he was also supported by his ex-girlfriend Kari Byron. Kari stated that the allegations were stupid and ignorant. Tory and Kari were co-stars in the show Mythbusters. They did very well to hide that they were girlfriend and boyfriend, back when they were dating. It looks like Tory is a man who likes to keep his love affairs a secret and away from the media. Well, the former couple didn’t share anything about them, but it looks like they have managed to maintain a good relationship as friends even after the split. Now, Kari Byron is happily married to their husband Paul Urich for 10 years.

Well, we don’t have much information about his current relationship status. However, there were rumors of him dating a woman from his production house. But like his previous affair with Kari, Tory has done a magnificent job keeping it a secret.
