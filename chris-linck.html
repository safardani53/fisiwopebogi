Who is Chris Linck:

Chris Linck is a famous Guitarist. He was born on May 12, 1992 and his birthplace is Atlanta, GA. Chris is also well known as, Lead guitarist for the metalcore band Attila, an Atlanta-based group who released 3 Billboard 200 charting albums between 2011 and 2014, including the 2013 album About That Life which peaked at #22 on the chart.

Chris is originated from United States. He performs alongside original member and frontman Chris Fronzak in Attila.
