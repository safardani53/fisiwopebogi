Tom Cruise goes undercover on night out with girlfriend at Winter Wonderland

HE is best known for going undercover as Ethan Hunt in the Mission: Impossible films.

And Tom Cruise pulled off another brilliant deception as he mingled with the public during a top-secret date with his Russian girlfriend.

Insiders revealed the Hollywood superstar and Elsina Khayrova put on their best disguises to visit London’s festive Winter Wonderland.

It features an ice rink, fairground rides and a Bavarian-style beer hall.

A source said: “With all their millions, Tom and Elsina can live the high life and never have to be near the public.

"But since they’ve been in London, they have been going out and about without anyone noticing them.

“Last week they went on a secret date to Winter Wonderland so they could see the attractions.

“It is a really romantic spot for couples — and Tom and Elsina blended in perfectly.

“He had people on hand to ensure they could be whisked away if they were spotted but no one batted an eyelid.

“They walked around all the attractions and took it all in, just like everyone else.”

Tom and Elsina are gearing up for their first Christmas as a couple after their romance was made public earlier this month.

He has pulled out all the stops to impress Elsina, and on Friday hired the entire floor of Novikov in Mayfair — one of London’s poshest restaurants — in a bid to impress her.

Socialite Elsina is the daughter of Russian MP Rinat Khayrov and was first seen with Tom at a dinner dance in the capital a few weeks ago.

Like Tom, she has had her share of high-profile romances and she was also embroiled in a messy split from diamond tycoon Dmitry Tsvetkov three years ago.

The pair, who were married for a decade and lived in a £22million mansion in Surrey, saw their divorce play out in the High Court, where Elsina was accused of trying to hide a stash of her designer handbags.

We’re sure Tom could buy her a few more.

SAM IS ON WHAM! TAIL

SAM RYDER is neck and neck with Wham! in their fight for the festive No1.

His Amazon exclusive track You’re Christmas To Me is at No2 after two days of sales, only 42 copies behind 1986 classic Last Christmas.

Sam has kicked things into overdrive promoting the track, performing on a bus which was turned into Santa’s grotto and driven on to London’s South Bank on Saturday.

The previous evening he had also sung the tune on TV’s The One Show as he launched his full charm offensive.

But Wham!’s team has also upped the ante by re-releasing Last Christmas on vinyl. 

The duo are loads of sales in front of Mariah Carey at No3 with All I Want For Christmas Is You. 

Despite being an early favourite at the bookies, Creator Universe, a group of British TikTok stars, are only at No11 with I Wish It Could Be Christmas Everyday.

Although there were hopes the Pogues would top the charts with Fairytale Of New York following frontman Shane MacGowan's death last month, it is at No5 according to the latest figures.

Noah Kahan’s Stick Season and Jack Harlow's Lovin On Me are the only non-festive hits in the Top 20.

Sam said on Saturday: “Imagine blasting through the sky on a runaway sleigh with a skin full of mulled wine next to the big red guy...That’s my Christmas song.”

With four days still to go, anything could happen.

Rita feels Ora right for next album

RITA ORA says one of her biggest achievements of the year is “making it through emotionally, mentally and physically”.

Despite having a hectic schedule that makes us tired just looking at it, the Let You Love Me singer says she has tried to put her mental wellbeing first and tone down her diary over the past 12 months.

Rita told Spotify: “I’ve been prioritising my mental health, working out and not being as crazy and focused on other things and trying to stay down a clean path.”

But don’t think that means 2024 will be quiet for Rita, because she will be working on her fourth album in January.

She explained: “I am really excited. I just want to keep going now. I don’t want to stop.

“I am doing a couple of films next year too.”

There’s no stopping her.

Demi engaged

ZO-NING IN TO LONDON

CHANNING TATUM and Zoe Kravitz have celebrated their engagement with a low-key visit to London.

Our exclusive snap shows them holding hands on a date in the capital at the weekend.

Onlookers spotted them wandering through Soho, then stopping off for dinner at Dirty Bones, which dishes up American-style comfort food.

One said: “Zoe and Channing looked so happy together and were oblivious to the people around them. They are huge stars but didn’t seem to mind who saw them out and about together.

“It was lovely to see they were able to just head out for dinner like any other young couple.”

Magic Mike star Channing and X-Men: First Class actress Zoe have been together for two years and since their engagement Zoe has been showing off her impressive diamond ring.

Though they like to be low-key, I can imagine their wedding will be a pretty star-studded affair.

Told you, Olly is our Euro star

YOU read it here first, and now Olly Alexander has confirmed he will represent the UK at the Eurovision Song Contest next May.

Last month we told how the Years & Years singer, inset, was in advanced talks to take part in the competition in Malmo, Sweden.

Olly confirmed our tale and said: “I’ve loved watching Eurovision since I was a little kid and I’m beyond excited to be taking part.

“I really can’t believe I’m going to be a part of such a special legacy and fly the flag for the UK in the gayest way possible. It shall be an honour.”

Olly added: “I’ve wanted to do this for a long time and it feels like the right moment to start releasing music under my own name.

“I’m determined to give it everything I’ve got and put on an excellent and unforgettable performance for you all.”

We’ve no doubt at all that Olly will smash it.
