Who is Dorothy Vaughan:

Dorothy Vaughan is a famous Mathematician. She was born on September 20, 1910 and her birthplace is Missouri. Dorothy is also well known as, Mathematician most well known for her work with the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics, the predecessor to the National Aeronautics and Space Administration. She would become the first African-American woman to be head of personnel at the NACA.

Dorothy is originated from United States. In 2016, the film Hidden Figures documented her career at NASA. She was depicted by actress Octavia Spencer.
