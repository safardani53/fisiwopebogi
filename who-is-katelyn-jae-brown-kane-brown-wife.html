Who Is Kane Brown's Wife? All About Katelyn Jae Brown

Kane Brown met his wife, Katelyn Jae Brown, just as he was becoming a huge country star.

The pair connected in 2016 at one of the "Heaven" singer's first concerts. They quickly bonded over their shared passion for music and got married in 2018. Kane and Katelyn welcomed their first daughter, Kingsley, in 2019 and their second, Kodi, in 2021. On Christmas Day in 2023, the couple revealed they're expecting their third child.

She has collaborated on music projects with Kane

Katelyn and Kane have worked together on music throughout their relationship. In 2020, she appeared with the couple's daughter, Kingsley, in the music video for Kane's song, "Worship You."

She and Kane performed their duet at the CMT Awards and Stagecoach in 2023

The country couple took their talents to the CMT Awards stage in April 2023 to perform their duet "Thank God" together. After their sweet performance, in which they donned matching black outfits, the couple took home the biggest award of the night, Video of the Year, for their collaboration on the "Thank God" music video.

"This is all so new to me and when we recorded this song a year ago I never in my life would ever think that this was ever going to happen," Katelyn said while thanking Kane in her acceptance speech. "This is so special that we get to share this together every night. I love you so much."

Later that month, Kane and Katelyn performed together at Stagecoach in Indio, California.

She and Kane attended the 2023 ACM Honors

In August 2023, the couple attended The Academy of Country Music Honors in Nashville, where Kane was honored with the ACM International Award.

"I want to thank my beautiful wife, who has been with me from the start. You're superwoman and I love you so much," Kane said during his acceptance speech that evening.
