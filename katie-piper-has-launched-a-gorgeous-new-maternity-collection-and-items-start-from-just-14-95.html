Katie Piper has launched a gorgeous new maternity collection... and items start from just 14.95

KATIE Piper has revealed her new range of maternity clothing and all of the items are selling at affordable prices.

The collection, called Mother By Katie Piper, is the presenter’s second clothing collection with online retailer WantThatTrend.com.

The new maternity collection includes 26 pieces in a variety of colours and prints to reflect Katie’s own personal style.

The range includes pretty skater, bodycon, wrap over and midi dresses – as well as a selection of T-shirts and jumpsuits.

Each piece was carefully chosen by Katie and the Want That Trend team to incorporate style and comfort with an affordable price point.

The clothes are available in sizes 6-18 and hopes to let women of a range of sizes embrace their growing bump and changing body shape.

Stand-out pieces of the collection include the Bardot Twist Front Detailed Dress, £24.95, the Tube Detailed Maxi Dress, £22.95, and the 3/4 Sleeve Wrap Over Belted Dress, £24.95.

What’s more, £1 from each sale will be donated to The Katie Piper Foundation - to support their work with burns survivors.

Katie said: “I wanted launch my own maternity range as soon as I found out the wonderful news that I was expecting my second child.

“Throughout my first pregnancy I struggled to find any maternity clothes that I liked and couldn’t believe how expensive maternity clothes were – especially as you are only able to wear them for a few months!

“Pregnancy is a time where our body hang-ups become exaggerated and our confidence is tested, so I hope that other women are able to embrace the changes to the body that pregnancy brings and feel gorgeous in my new ‘Mother’ maternity collection.”

Victoria Molyneux, Founder of Want That Trend.Com, added: “Katie and I wanted to work together to provide a maternity collection that would allow women expecting to feel fantastic and confident at such a special time in their lives.”
