Jessica Biel, American Actress, Producer

Jessica Biel is an American actress and producer. She rose to fame playing Mary Camden on the TV series 7th Heaven, which ran from 1996 to 2006. Biel's most famous films include Ulee's Gold (1997), The Texas Chainsaw Massacre (2003), Blade: Trinity (2004), The Illusionist (2006) and Total Recall (2012). She also produced and starred in Hulu's 2022 crime series Candy. In 2018, she was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award for outstanding lead actress for The Sinner anthology series.
