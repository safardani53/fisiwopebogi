Frozen 2 Photos Of Anna &amp; Elsa Hollywood Life

The Disney sequel we’ve all been waiting for is happening. ‘Frozen 2’ will make its highly-anticipated debut in theaters on Nov. 22. The sequel comes nearly 6 years after the release of ‘Frozen,’ which debuted on November 27, 2013. The film went on to make over $1.2 billion at the box office, making it the highest-grossing animated film of all-time. Idina Menzel and Kristen Bell voice the roles of Elsa and Anna. They’re back again in the sequel for a new adventure, along with Jonathan Groff’s Kristoff and Josh Gad’s Olaf. There will be new songs, costumes, and adventures. The ‘Frozen’ crew will be heading off to enchanted lands in search of the truth. The first teaser trailer was released in Feb. 2019 with the full-length trailer debuting in June 2019. ‘Frozen 2’ will be released in theaters on Nov. 22.
