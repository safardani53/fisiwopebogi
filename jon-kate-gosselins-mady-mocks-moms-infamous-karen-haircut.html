Jon and Kate Gosselin's daughter Mady, 20, mocks mom's infamous 'Karen' haircut from show's early ye

JON and Kate Gosselin's 20-year-old daughter Mady mocked her mom's infamous "Karen" haircut from the show's early years in a new TikTok family.

The famous family starred on Jon & Kate Plus 8 on TLC from 2007-2017.

Mady took to TikTok to poke fun at Kate, 45, in the video, which was posted on Friday.

The clip began with text appearing onscreen next to Mady that read: "Disrespect my mom?"

She then shared the searched results for the word "Karen" on Google, which featured two throwback photos of her mother at the top.

After zooming in on a photo of Kate to show off the hairstyle, Mady turned that camera onto herself to show she was laughing.

The video was set to the song Poke It Out by Nicki Minaj and Playboi Carti.

Mady has created quite the following on the social media platform and regularly posts fun videos.

Jon, 43, and Kate were married from 1999-2009.

The former couple are the parents to  20-year-old twins Mady and Cara and 16-year-old sextuplets Alexis, Aeden, Hannah, Collin, Leah, and Joel.

Hannah and Collin live with Jon and his girlfriend Colleen Conrad, while the rest of the children live with Kate.

Jon recently revealed that he was hospitalized with Covid-19 while appearing on The Dr. Oz Show.

After detailing his experience in the hospital, Jon said that the "brush with death" made him reevaluate his relationship with his children.

When asked if he had a message for his estranged kids, Jon said he wished he could tell his estranged children that he loves them and that he's "just thinking of future more."

He added: "I hope they develop a relationship with Hannah and Collin. I hope that one day I can be part of your life. I'm glad that I got a second chance."
