Gegard Mousasi (MMA Fighter) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Gegard Mousasi, a Dutch mixed martial artist and former kickboxer, currently competes in Bellator MMA, where he is a two-time and current Bellator middleweight champion. On March 29, 2021, he placed 3rd in the Bellator men's pound-for-pound ranking. He's also a former DREAM lightweight champion, former DREAM mid-size champion, former Cage Warriors mid-size champion and former Strikeforce heavyweight champion, making Mousasi a six-time MMA world champion. By the time he left the UFC in July 2017, he was ranked 4th in the official UFC ranking for average height, and he is currently ranked 3rd among MMA boxers and is ranked 9th by weight above the world according to the Matrix Fight.
