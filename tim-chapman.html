Tim Chapman Biography

Tim Chapman is a former bounty hunter from the United States. He is mainly popular these days as a reality show star and is well known for his appearance in the show A&E TV's Dog the Bounty Hunter.

Tim Chapman Birth, Education, Age

Tim Chapman was born Timothy Charles Chapman on 13 May 1965 in Ventura, California, the United States of America to Diane Wimberly and Ronald Chapman. He is also known by the nickname Youngblood. He is of white ethnicity.

How old is Tim Chapman?

As of 2019, he is 54 years of age.

Marriage, Divorce, & Children With Wife Davina Chapman 

Tim was previously married to Davina Chapman whom he dated for long before they held their wedding. Davina Chapman is a lovely lady and as a married couple, they welcomed three children together. They got divorced in 2009

He also had a son named Tim Chapman Jr., from his previous relationship. His nationality is American and his ethnicity is white.

Is Tim Chapman Dating a Girlfriend?

As of 2019, Tim is single and not dating a girlfriend, but in the past, he was in a romantic relationship with Beth Smith. The duo remained in a relationship for quite a while and broke up.

Apart from that, he has not mentioned dating a girlfriend. Further, he has also not planned for getting married for the second time as well.

Legal Issues

At the end of 2006, he along with Duane "Dog" Chapman and Leland Chapman were arrested by U.S. Marshals. They were sent to Mexico to face charges. He was released on bail of $100,000 each. He also got arrested for the second time in his life in 2008

How Much is Tim Chapman Net Worth?

Though his exact salary is unknown, his net worth is reported to be more than $3 million in 2019. His fortune is expected to increase further in the coming years.
