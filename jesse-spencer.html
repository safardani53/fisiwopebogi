Jesse Spencer Age & Biography

Jesse Spencer was born on February 12, 1979, and is currently 45 years old in 2023. He was born in the Australian city of Melbourne. He is of Australian nationality and follows the Jewish faith. His zodiac sign is Aquarius, and he is of Australian American descent.

Jesse finished his education at Canterbury Primary School. He would thereafter begin his schooling at Malvern Central School in Australia.
