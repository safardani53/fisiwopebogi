The Miley Cyrus Clothing Line!

Clearly a fashion icon, Miley Cyrus has teamed up with Max Azria on a clothing line.

The singer/actress gave People a preview of it this week, as everything from her plaid shirts to high-waisted shorts will soon be on sale at Walmart.

“I wanted to finally make something that was for girls my age. Something me and my friends would wear,” Miley said. “But I’d say anyone from age 8-80 can enjoy this line.”

Would you wear any of these Miley Cyrus-designed clothes?

Cyrus added that she wants her U.S. fans to dress more like those across the Atlantic.

“My inspiration for the line is kind of an edgy UK style,” she said. “Every time I go to Europe I love going to all the funky stores. I wanted to be able to bring those fun styles to the girls of America.”

No word yet on when or if Miley will fashion her own line of hooker boots, based on the pair she wore for a recent Elle photo shoot.
