Who is Debra Martin Chase:

Debra Martin Chase is a famous Film Producer. She was born on October 11, 1956 and her birthplace is Illinois. Debra is also well known as, American film and television producer whose company, Martin Chase Productions, worked closely with Disney and ABC. She was the first African-American woman to have a solo producing deal with a major studio.

Debra is originated from United States. She produced The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants, which starred America Ferrera.
