Who is Kristyn Osborn:

Kristyn Osborn is a famous Country Singer. She was born on August 24, 1970 and her birthplace is Magna, UT. Kristyn is also well known as, Singer-songwriter most famous as the primary songwriter for the all-sister country band SHeDAISY. With SHeDAISY, she has released platinum-selling albums like the group’s 1999 debut “The Whole SHeBANG.”

Kristyn is originated from United States. She dated “The Dark Knight” actor Aaron Eckhart from 2006 to 2007.
