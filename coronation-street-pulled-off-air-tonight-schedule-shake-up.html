Coronation Street pulled off air tonight in ITV schedule shake-up - leaving fans livid

CORONATION Street has been pulled off air tonight in an ITV schedule shake-up - leaving fans livid.

The soap usually airs for one hour on a Monday from 8pm until 9pm.

But this week, tonight's episode has been removed from the schedules because of the football.

The FA Cup enters the third round and the match airing tonight will see Wigan Athletic take on Manchester United.

Coverage starts from 7.30pm with the match kicking off at 8pm, and it means Coronation Street has to air its quota of episodes later in the week.

Soap fans will get three one-hour episodes on Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday this week to make up for the fact it won't be on tonight.

But some weren't happy, with one writing on X: "no #Corrie tonight.Football instead."

Another had tweeted last week: "What's #Corrie doing on today? not more rugby, football whatever."

Unfortunately, it isn't the first time Corrie fans faced disruption due to the football.

Last Friday, the soap was missing from schedules due to the match between Tottenham Hotspur and Burnley.
