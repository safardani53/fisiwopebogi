15 best reusable shopping bags you can buy in 2022

IF YOU'RE committed to helping the environment and want to cut down on your plastic shopping bag use, you should invest in one of the best reusable shopping bags around.

They are usually made of cotton, nylon or mesh and have emerged as the stylish alternative that's better for the environment than single-use bags, as well as much more convenient.

Reusable shopping bags come in a range of styles, prints and price points. In fact, they've become style statements in their own right.

They're also more durable than plastic bags, which are prone to rips, holes or breakages, and often more spacious.

From cotton totes to mesh bags, these are the best reusable shopping bags available on the market to buy now.

How to fold reusable shopping bags

How to fold reusable shopping bags is entirely dependent on which fabric shopper you've chosen.

If it's cotton, mesh or nylon, it usually can scrunch into a small ball or pouch and be chucked straight into your handbag.

A lot of reusable shopping bags come with attached pouches, which can be tricky to fold down - we recommend laying it on a flat surface, folding the sides to meet in the middle, and then rolling up tightly from the bottom.

If your shopping is jute, or wipe-clean plastic (like a Bag for Life) it is best to follow the pre-folded lines when it comes to storage.

Pinch the sides of the bag to collapse it, then fold up the bottom, and then in half to store with ease.

Can you recycle reusable shopping bags?

If you have a supermarket-branded reusable shopping bag, you can often take it in-store to a plastic bag recycling point to be recycled, and then replaced free of charge.

If you have opted for a fabric bag from this list, it can often be recycled in a fabric recycling drop-off facility - usually found at your local supermarket next to the bottle bins.

Be sure to check with the brand, recycling point or supermarket before recycling to make extra sure your bag can be recycled.

Can you wash reusable shopping bags?

Most reusable shopping bags are thankfully machine washable. If you've opted for a cotton, mesh, string, or nylon reusable shopper, then it can be chucked in the machine to wash at (an eco-friendly!) 30 degrees.

If you have opted for jute or plastic, it is often wipe-clean with soap and warm water.

Always be sure to check the tag or with the brand your bag is from before washing.

How to dispose of reusable shopping bags

Most shopping bags are extremely long-lasting, and a lot of brands boast they'll last a lifetime - so hopefully you won't need to chuck them out if you take good care of them.

If you can, repair any ripped shopping bags with a simple stitch, or donate to charity if a bag is still in good condition.

Otherwise, you can recycle fabric bags at a fabric recycling facility or plant - usually found at supermarkets.
