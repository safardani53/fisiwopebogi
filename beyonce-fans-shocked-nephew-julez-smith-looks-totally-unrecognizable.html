Beyonce fans shocked as nephew Julez Smith, 18, looks totally unrecognizable and 'all grown up' in v

Beyonce fans were shocked by how grown up her nephew Julez Smith looked in a new photo.

Julez Smith, 18, is the son of the R&B legend's sister Solange Knowles, who gave birth to her son at the age of 17.

The photo was shared on Instagram by Beyonce, 41, and Solange's mother, Tina Knowles-Lawson, 68.

Captioned, "My grandbaby is all grown up," the post shows two side-by-side photos of Tina and 18-year-old Julez.

Both grandmother and grandson don formal attire, she wears an evening gown while Julez sports a pinstripe suit.

The first photo depicts the 18-year-old kissing Tina on the cheek with the second showing the mother of two and a mature-looking Julez smiling at the camera.

TEEN MOM

Actress/Singer Solange Knowles, 36, gave birth to Julez in 2004 at the age of 17.

The year proved to be trying for the young mother for several reasons.

In a reflective letter to her past self for Teen Vogue, Knowles opened up about the difficult times she faced.

"Seventeen will be the hardest year of your life," she wrote in 2017.

"It will grow you up almost immediately.

"You will lose your best friend whom you love so much to gun violence in a single moment, and give birth to a new one within a year."

Her childhood friend, Marsai Song, was killed in a drive-by shooting that year.

She continued: "You will be terrified, and it’s ok that you don’t know what the future holds.

"Some people will count you out because of the decision you’ve made to bring another life into the world so young, but you made the decision out of love and will live with the decision in love."

She welcomed her first child, son Daniel Julez J. Smith Jr., with Daniel Smith in October 2004.

They also got tied the knot that year but would divorce in 2007.

Smith is Knowles' only child.
