How to make... A rhubarb & ginger spritzer

An aperitif is especially popular as we head into warmer weather. Here's how to make a Rhubarb & Ginger Spritzer with Aldi's award-winning gin...

Ingredients:

  • 50ml Haysmith’s Rhubarb & Ginger Gin
  • 25ml Cranberry Juice
  • 25ml Apple Juice
  • 75ml Specially Selected Organic Prosecco
  • Squeeze of fresh lime
  • Orange peel for garnish

Method:

  • Set the Prosecco aside and shake the other ingredients with ice
  • Strain into a glass, top with Prosecco and garnish with the orange peel. Voila!
