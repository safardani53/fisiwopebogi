Middlesbrough vs Millwall: Live stream, TV channel, kick-off time and team news for Championship pro

MIDDLESBROUGH and Millwall are both battling to secure their spot in the Championship play-offs.

Tony Pulis' squad enter the clash on the back of two league victories, while the Lions look to bounce back from a 3-0 defeat to Fulham.

The visitors are just three points behind Boro after 44 games.

What time is kick-off?

THIS match will take place on Saturday, April 28.

It will kick-off at 5:30pm.

Riverside Stadium will host the showdown.

Which TV channel is it on and can I live stream it?

THIS match will be shown live on Sky Sports Football and Sky Sports Main Event.
