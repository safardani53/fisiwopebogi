Stacy Keibler Talks George Clooney Split, Healthy Dessert Recipes

She may have broken up with one of the sexiest men alive, but Stacy Keibler isn’t broken up about her George Clooney split.

“There’s nothing really dramatic that happened, so it’s all good,” Keibler told Extra this week of her and Clooney’s collapse, which reportedly took place over the phone.

“I’m someone that’s always lived the present moment. I always look at the positive on everything,” Keibler added. “I’m so excited for all these projects coming up, and I’m super busy.”

That’s for sure.

 

Keibler starts hosting Supermarket Superstar on Lifetime this Monday, a series that “gives home cooks the chance to pitch their recipe ideas in the hopes of getting a nationwide launch of their product on supermarket shelves,” the beauty explains.

In the end, one contestant will earn the opportunity to create a multi-million dollar line.

Keibler says she was a big fan of The Food Network growing up, making Supermarket Superstar a “natural fit” in her career progression.

The host said she specializes in making healthy dessert recipes that are sugar-free, gluten-free and even dairy-free.

“They’re healthy, so everyone can indulge without worrying about their waistline,” she said.

Sounds delicious to us!
