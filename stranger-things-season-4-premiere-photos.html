Stranger Things 4 Premiere: Photos Hollywood Life

Fond farewell! ‘Stranger Things’ stars and creators celebrated the final season of the show with the premiere of season 4 in New York on Saturday, May 14. The stars, most of whom started the show as kids, looked all grown up while rocking an array of stylish looks on the red carpet. 

Star Millie Bobby Brown dished about the season, telling fans season 4 ‘definitely [has] lots of answers. She added there’s ‘lots of crying, lots of laughing’ too.

Here, the whole ‘Stranger Things’ team gathers for a photo. Pictured: Cara Buono, Maya Hawke, Winona Ryder, Campbell Bower, Millie Bobby Brown, Matthew Modine, Caleb McLaughlin, Brett Gelman, Finn Wolfhard, Eduardo Franco, Sadie Sink, Noah Schnapp, Joseph Quinn, Gaten Matarazzo, Charlie Heaton, Priah Ferguson, David Harbour, Natalia Dyer and Joe Keery.
