Im a size 18 &amp; my besties a size 2 - we tried the same bathing suits, they look great on both

TWO friends with different body shapes have shared how great they look in the same interchangeable bathing suits during a recent haul.

The women took to TikTok to stun viewers with how well-fitting and comfortable the same swimsuits looked while they wore them in different sizes.

The Lemon Drop Shop, known as @lemondropshop on the platform, is a women-owned clothing boutique for women in sizes S to size 5X.

The southern Virginia-based clothing store is owned by Kayla, a wife, and mother who wanted a place where women could find affordable stylish clothing for all sizes. 

In the 19-second video, two women try on four size-inclusive swimsuits in a haul from the brand that showcases the diversity of the store and the beauty of different body shapes.

According to the store website, Kayla opened the shop because of her love for fashion and for making women feel as beautiful as they truly are.

The video begins with one blonde woman smiling in front of the camera before packing up to show two blonde women and their sizes overhead.

Show Stopper by Danity Kane is casually playing in the background of the video.

The phrase "Same Outfit Different Size Swim Edition!" is written in the center of the visual before disappearing to show the sizing differences between the women.

The entire video is a sequence of movements and dances that effortlessly transition into each ensemble.

The woman on the left is wearing a Size 16/18 which equates to an XL in the store's clothes and the woman on the right is a size 2/4, which is a size small.

The first swim set the women present to viewers is a leopard print high-waisted tankini set that cinches in at the waist to give a slimming effect.

With a snap of their fingers, the women then change into the next interchangeable bathing suit and swap out the animal print top for a solid black thick spaghetti strap bikini crop top with a tie feature in the front.

The crop top lifts and supports both women in the chest without being too much and covers common problem areas.

They then move out of the frame to exchange the leopard print bottoms and pop back in with a fun pair of high-waisted black and white vertical striped women's swim bottoms.

Two seconds later they "drop it low and bring it up slow" to show the black bathing suit top now paired with plain black high-waisted bottoms.

Both women are wearing matching black and white lace beach cover-ups to contrast with the all-black two-pieces.

To tie the video together, the swimsuit models then spin into the last look which is the original leopard print swimsuit top paired with the black bottoms.

All-in-all, the video exemplifies the fact that sometimes less is more and you can do so much with very little.

The key is to be resourceful and it seems like the Lemon Drop Shop has the fashion tastes to suit a number of style needs and body types.

Many viewers felt the same and the comment section reflected that.
