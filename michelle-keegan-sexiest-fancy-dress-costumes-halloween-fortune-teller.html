Michelle Keegans most stunning fancy dress costumes - including THAT Halloween fortune teller out

SHE’S one of the sexiest women in Britain and also knows how to pull off an epic fancy dress outfit too. 

Michelle Keegan has done a number of incredible costumes over the years, which look like perfect inspiration for this Halloween....

Fortune teller

One of her most jaw-dropping Halloween looks was a fortune teller ensemble, which also gave off serious pirate vibes. 

The former Corrie star added a headscarf and jewels to her 2018 outfit, and accessorised with hoop earrings. 

To really stand out, she also wore striking contact lenses which make her eyes pop. 

Fans praised her look on Instagram, with one saying: “Why is she always so beautiful no matter what.”

Rizzo from Grease

In 2015, Michelle teamed up with husband Mark Wright as they celebrated Halloween out in Essex. 

The pair stole the show as Kenickie and Rizzo from Grease, complete with the famous jackets from the T-Birds and the Pink Ladies. 

Michelle and Mark added a spooky twist to their outfits by applying some fake blood. We love it!

Marilyn Monroe

Michelle channeled the sex goddess Marilyn Monroe herself last year when she dressed up in the famous white dress. 

The outfit wasn’t strictly for Halloween, as Michelle wore the iconic blonde wig to ring in 2020 at New Year, but it was incredible fancy dress inspiration nonetheless. 

Keeping with the Marilyn theme, Mark added a wig and dark make-up to become Marilyn Manson. 

The Incredibles

Michelle and Mark had previously teamed up to become Mr and Mrs Incredible for a fancy dress party last December. 

The glamorous pair showed off their swanky Essex pad as they posed in their tight-fitting red crime-fighting outfits by their Christmas tree. 

The couple completed their superhero look with the characters’ black masks. 

Schoolgirl

Way back in 2012, Michelle hit the town clubbing dressed as a schoolgirl, complete with knee-high socks and ribbons in her hair. 

The event was for Coronation Street star Kym Marsh celebrating a second hen do with a host of her soap star pals dressed in the ‘back to school’ theme. 

Michelle completed the look with a backpack which she carried to the Circle Club in Manchester.

Chav party

Michelle wore leggings and tacky gold jewellery for her “chav party” as she waved goodbye to her Coronation cast in 2014. 

The theme was in honour of her streetwise character Tina McIntyre, and Michelle certainly looked the part. 

The leaving party took place in Manchester, where cast and crew partied in their themed get-up. 

Robin

Michelle got her superhero back in 2008 when she dressed up as Batman’s sidekick Robin. 

She was pictured arriving at Kim Ryder’s 30th birthday party at the Manchester 235 Casino.

The stunning soap star pulled out all the stops wearing green tights and gold boots.

There would have been no missing Michelle in this colourful ensemble, which had a large ‘R’ emblem on her bright tunic.
