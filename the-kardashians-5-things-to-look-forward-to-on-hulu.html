The Kardashians- 5 Things to Look Forward To On Hulu

The Kardashians are set to premiere on Hulu in April, providing fans with the long-awaited drama that has been missing since the end of KUWTK in 2020.

Hulu announced in December 2020 that the family had signed a long-term deal to create a new show for the streaming service. The Kardashian-Jenner family will reemerge on screens with their own Hulu/Disney+ series.

The finale of Keeping Up With the Kardashians undoubtedly left many fans disillusioned and heartbroken. However, fans can rest assured that the Kardashian-Jenner saga is far from done. The Kardashians have said their goodbyes to Keeping Up With the Kardashians after a 20-season run and are gearing up for a new adventure.

Kim Kardashian said on Instagram that the Kardashian family has decided to terminate the famous reality show in September 2020. In her caption, the founder of Skims and KKW Fragrance said,

“We’ve made the painful decision as a family to say goodbye to Keeping Up with the Kardashians with sorrowful hearts.”

She went on to say,

“We are eternally grateful to those of you who have stood with us through the good times, the difficult times, the smiles, the tears, and the various relationships and children we’ve had over the years. We’ll treasure the amazing memories we’ve made and the innumerable people we’ve met along the route for the rest of our lives.”

Many more facts about The Kardashians are slowly emerging, making fans impatient with the constant tease. So, here’s everything they need to know about the drama that’s about to hit their television screens.

Before Hulu’s The Kardashians release, we’re digging into the details.

1) The return of all of the Kardashian-Jenner sisters, as well as their mother.

The new Hulu’s The Kardashians trailer depicts all six of the Kardashian-Jenner sisters, despite Kourtney Kardashian’s previous comments that she is done with the show. Kris Jenner, the boss-mom, is confirmed as a cast member of The Kardashians, as are Kourtney, Kim, Khloé, Kendall, and Kylie, who all appeared in the teaser dressed to the nines.

While no additional KUWTK cast members have confirmed their participation in the Hulu show, we can expect to see Scott Disick, Rob Kardashian, and the rest of the Kar-Jenner children.

According to rumors, the family will have complete control over the show’s editing, and fans can expect to learn more about Kim’s business ventures and educational history.

Kris Jenner, the boss-lady, said in May 2021, while promoting the Hulu series at the Disney Upfront showcase,

“You’ll watch us grow as a family in the upcoming show. Fans expect us to be ourselves, and they’ve been emotionally immersed in our show since the beginning, just like we have. The fans will be ecstatic to see us continue on our adventure. I can’t reveal too much about what’s to come, but spoiler alert: we’re going to look amazing, and everyone will be watching.”

2) Scott Disick is going to be a part of the drama.

According to US Weekly, Scott Disick, Kourtney’s ex-husband, agreed to feature in the Hulu series after being promised a large quantity of money. Although Scott hasn’t confirmed anything, fans are curious to see how Scott, Kourtney, and Kourtney’s current fiancé, Travis Barker, deal with their feud.

Scott Disick will star on the show, according to Kim Kardashian, who tweeted about it in May 2021.

However, there have been reports concerning Disick’s involvement in the forthcoming Hulu series. Scott is said to have unfollowed all Kardashians after being booted off the reality show.

3) A clear look into the private lives of the family

The Kardashian-Jenner sisters and other family members will continue to be as open about their personal lives as they were on the previous program, KUWTK. To everyone’s delight, however, programs about such events will be broadcast closer to the time they occurred.

Kim and Kanye West’s breakup and her romance with Pete Davidson will be covered on The Kardashians. The show will reveal juicy details about Khloé’s relationship with Tristan Thompson, including the emotional aftermath after his child with another woman was discovered.

The show will also capture Kylie Jenner and Kendall Jenner’s lives and Kourtney’s romance with artist Travis Barker. Caitlyn Jenner will not participate in the show, while Rob Kardashian only makes brief cameos.

4) Travis Baker will appear in the family drama as Kourtney’s fiancé.

According to a video uploaded on Instagram by the show’s official page, legendary drummer Travis Barker will appear in The Kardashians. In the tape, viewers watched clips from Kourtney and Travis’ engagement and Kris’ poignant tribute to her daughter and Baker. Fans have speculated that the show will highlight the tensions between Disick and Barker.

While neither has made an official announcement, fans will have to wait for the cast members to tease more official information.

In addition, Travis Barker, Kourtney’s current fiancé, will participate in the show, according to Kim. Kim announced to Ellen DeGeneres in September 2021 that:

“I haven’t talked to them about it,” she says, “but I’m expecting they will be on because he’s such an important part of Kourtney’s life.”

5) On the margins of the reality TV drama series, Rob Kardashian

A key member of the Kardashian-Jenner family will be absent from the forthcoming episode. According to sources, Rob Kardashian will not appear in the upcoming Hulu series.

Due to his desire to live a life away from the spotlight, the 34-year-old Kardashian brother opted out of the show. Nonetheless, he is expected to play a supporting role on the sidelines.

Despite having appeared in multiple episodes of KUWTK over the last few years, Rob has opted to take a break from the show. According to sources and stories, Rob has also been focusing on spending more time with his own family.

According to an unnamed insider who spoke to E! News,

“Rob is in a fantastic situation. He’s content to live a quiet life in Calabasas, away from the limelight.”

According to the source,

“Rob has no plans to appear on the new Hulu show, but he believes he’s in a terrific headspace right now.”

The Kardashians will launch on Hulu on April 14 and will mark the start of a new chapter for the legendary family.

Join them on their life adventures as they dish out the juiciest drama and gossip from their private lives.
